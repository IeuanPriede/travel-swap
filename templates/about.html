Our Mission

TravelSwap was born out of a love for authentic travel experiences. We believe that exploring the world doesn't have to mean expensive hotels or feeling like a tourist. With TravelSwap, you can swap homes with like-minded travelers, live like a local, and build lasting connections.

How It Works

Simply create a profile, list your home, and start browsing. Like what you see? Click like 👍! If both parties like each other’s homes, it’s a match—and you can message directly to arrange your travel exchange.

Who We Are

We’re a small, passionate team of travelers and tech enthusiasts who believe in meaningful travel and mutual trust. Whether it’s a beachside bungalow, a mountain retreat, or a city apartment—we help you find the perfect place to stay, your way.
